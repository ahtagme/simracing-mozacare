============================================================
TIPS PAKAI WHATSAPP BUSINESS
============================================================

1. Daftar WhatsApp Business (gratis di Play Store/App Store)
   - Konversi nomor WA pribadi atau pakai nomor baru

2. Setup Quick Replies:
   - Settings > Business Tools > Quick replies
   - Add shortcut: /greeting, /price, /payment, /done, dll
   - Tinggal ketik shortcut, message langsung muncul

3. Setup Auto Reply:
   - Settings > Business Tools > Greeting message
   - Aktifkan: "Send to people who message you for the first time"
   - Paste template #1 di atas

4. Setup Away Message (jam offline):
   - Settings > Business Tools > Away message
   - Schedule: 22:00 - 09:00
   - Paste template #13

5. Catalog Produk:
   - Settings > Business Tools > Catalog
   - Add produk + harga
   - Customer bisa browse langsung di chat

6. Labels untuk track customer:
   - Buat label: "Pending Payment", "In Progress", "Done", "VIP"
   - Drag chat ke label sesuai status

7. Backup chat tiap minggu:
   - Settings > Chats > Chat backup > Backup to Google Drive
